OUR STORY

A journey to transparency

What began as a personal quest to create wearable fragrances—free from the allergens that made commercial perfumes impossible for me to enjoy—evolved into something much larger.

I discovered that most major fragrance brands hide behind proprietary formulas, listing only "fragrance" on their labels while masking dozens of undisclosed chemicals, many of them known irritants and hormone disruptors.

Highland Hermitage was born from a simple belief: luxury consumers deserve to know exactly what they're wearing. Each fragrance is handcrafted in small batches in Nashville, with every ingredient proudly listed and every process transparent.
